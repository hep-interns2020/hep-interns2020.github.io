The Arts Cohort!

Welcome to the Arts Cohort! Over the last four weeks, we’ve been developing useful skills to help us create and finalize our virtual performance. In our daily meetings, we discuss the details of the mini-projects, like using Canva or researching film shots that will prepare us for the final product. We’ve learned that art isn’t just one thing. It’s anything you want it to be. We all have a deeper understanding of graphic design, videography, and the importance of camera angles, and lighting to create a great quality video. Through our 9 am morning meetings and our 5 pm deadlines, we’ve managed to gain good communication skills, time management skills, and good organizational skills. For those of you who don’t know, our final project is a film/music video. We decided to use the song Seventeen by Alessia Cara. We chose to use this song because it resonates with who we are as a group. As teenagers, we are at that time in our lives where we can’t wait to be older. We wanted to show and represent that we shouldn't take time for granted, because before we know it we'll all be grown up. We all can’t wait until you all see the skills we’ve gained and improved altogether. Thank you and Enjoy!
